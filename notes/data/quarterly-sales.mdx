## Monthly recurring revenue

MRR climbed every month over the trailing year, from **$1.18M to $1.64M** — a
**38.9%** increase. The three Q1 months (Jan–Mar) sum to $4.64M of recurring
revenue; the remaining ~$180k of Q1 revenue is one-off onboarding and services.
