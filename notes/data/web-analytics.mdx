## Takeaway

<Callout type="info" title="The newsletter is the cheapest channel we have">
At 2,980 sessions and a conversion rate near 9%, the newsletter delivered roughly **260
signups** — about 42% of the period's total — from an audience a fraction the size of
organic. Doubling send cadence (within reason) is the highest-leverage experiment for next
sprint. Pair it with a pass on `/pricing`, where high intent meets the lowest bounce on the site.
</Callout>
